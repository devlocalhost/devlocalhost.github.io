laurel_updates

This website has 2 cool functions:

  1. Two short URLs that redirect to laurel_updates
    1. Redirects to the stable version: https://devlocalhost.github.io/lu
    2. Redirects to the beta version: https://devlocalhost.github.io/lubeta
  2. URL shortener that redirects to laurel_updates (recommended)
    • This function is the recommended method to share URLs from laurel_updates (a blog post, ROM, etc). This way, users will always be redirected to the working domain of laurel_updates. For more details, read this blog post: 26-11-2024 - [IMPORTANT, MUST READ] MAJOR ISSUES WITH HOSTING. Reviews feature temporarily removed.
    • How it works? Easy. Putting anything after https://devlocalhost.github.io/ will append it to the laurel_updates URL, and redirect you there! An example: https://devlocalhost.github.io/roms/LineageOS_15
